# Competitive Landscape & Build Research: "Infection"-Style Roblox Game (30p, hidden infected, knives vs guns)

*Compiled 2026-07-16. This report informs MASTER-PROMPT.md — the build should follow its conclusions.*

## 1. Competitive landscape — top comparables

### Murder Mystery 2 (Nikilis) — closest analog for "hidden killer with knives"
- **Scale:** ~25–27B lifetime visits, tens of thousands of CCU sustained (profitable.app estimates ~$200M+ lifetime revenue; treat third-party revenue numbers as rough). 12+ years of updates ([profitable.app](https://profitable.app/roblox/games/murder-mystery-2), [RoMonitor](https://romonitorstats.com/experience/142823291/)).
- **Round structure:** Small lobbies (~12: up to 10 Innocents + 1 Sheriff + 1 Murderer, sometimes a Hero). Map voted from 3 options pre-round. Round ends on timer expiry, murderer death, or full wipe ([MM2 Fandom](https://murder-mystery-2.fandom.com/wiki/Murder_Mystery_2), [Gamemodes](https://murder-mystery-2.fandom.com/wiki/Gamemodes)).
- **Monetization = the whole business:** knife/gun cosmetic skins in 9 rarity tiers, crates, animated "Chroma" godlies, and a **player-to-player trading economy** — rare items resell for $1,500–$3,000 on grey markets ([Eldorado](https://www.eldorado.gg/blog/mm2-values/), [bloxodes — 524 knives](https://bloxodes.com/wiki/murder-mystery-2/knives)). Also small conveniences (Radio pass 499 R$, cosmetic-only). **Lesson: cosmetic weapon-skin rarity ladder + trading is the proven revenue engine for knife games.**
- **Complaints:** murderer "camping" (guarding the dropped gun), spam-jumping to dodge, and endemic exploiters/scam trading ([MM2 forum thread](https://murder-mystery-2.fandom.com/f/p/4400000000000160191/r/4400000000000799689)). Our design (everyone armed, conversion mechanic) inherently fixes MM2's "innocents have nothing to do" boredom problem.

### Piggy (MiniToon)
- **Scale:** 14B+ visits, 12-player lobbies, rounds 5–15 min ([Piggy Fandom](https://piggy.fandom.com/wiki/Piggy_(Game)), [earnaldo comparison](https://earnaldo.com/blog/flee-the-facility-vs-piggy)).
- **Has a literal "Infection" mode:** killed survivors instantly respawn as infected piggies (no traps for converts); infected team must convert everyone ([Piggy Infection wiki](https://piggy.fandom.com/wiki/Infection)). Instant-respawn-as-infected keeps eliminated players playing — **critical retention mechanic to copy**.
- **Economy:** Piggy Tokens — 15 per escape, 5 per capture/kill, 2x-token gamepass; tokens buy cosmetic skins. Cosmetic-only monetization ([Skins wiki](https://piggy.fandom.com/wiki/Skins)).

### Flee the Facility (A.W. Apps)
- **Scale:** 5.5B visits, 88%+ rating. 1 Beast vs up to 4 survivors ([Roblox Fandom](https://roblox.fandom.com/wiki/A.W._Apps/Flee_the_Facility)).
- **Pacing details worth copying:** Beast held in a spawn room 15s (survivor head-start); 30s lobby intermission with map voting plates; Beast is deliberately handicapped (first-person locked, slows after swing/jump, can't crawl vents) to balance 1vMany. XP Boost gamepass is the top seller; levels unlock cosmetics ([earnaldo guide](https://earnaldo.com/blog/flee-the-facility)).

### Survive the Killer (Slyce Entertainment)
- **Scale:** 1B+ visits; killer gets ~3–4 min window; rounds 3–8 min total ([earnaldo](https://earnaldo.com/blog/survive-the-killer), [STK wiki](https://survivethekiller.wiki/)).
- **Monetization:** knife-crate economy (coins or Robux, card-flip reveal showing "what you could have gotten" — strong dopamine/regret loop), knife skins + trading values community ([Crates wiki](https://survive-the-killer-roblox.fandom.com/wiki/Crates), [ProGameGuides values](https://progameguides.com/roblox/roblox-stk-value-list-all-survive-the-killer-trading-values/)).

### Infection-genre games specifically
- **Infection (Frosted Studio):** rounds max 10 min; humans get guns/sentries/shields; 8 zombie classes with distinct HP/abilities (Normal 100HP, Fast 50HP+speed, Fire/Ice projectiles 15 dmg, Ghost near-invisible 150–200HP, Lasso pull, Boomer 150HP explode, Boss 450HP shield+hammer) ([Roblox Fandom](https://roblox.fandom.com/wiki/Frosted_Studio/Infection)). **Zombie class variety is how infection games keep the zombie side fun.**
- **Infection Gunfight (9thD Workshop, 2024):** ~115M visits in ~2 years — proof the niche still grows fast. Round opens with a few seconds of no zombies, then 5 players convert; zombies get a G-key sprint burst on cooldown ([Rolimon's](https://www.rolimons.com/game/17840058476), [namuwiki](https://en.namu.wiki/w/Infection%20Gunfight)). Featured in Roblox's "The Hunt: Mega Edition" event.
- **Michael's Zombies:** 226M visits, 91.9% rating (360k up / 32k down) — but it's PvE COD-Zombies-clone (perks, pack-a-punch), not infection PvP. Praised for community depth and restrained monetization; older-skewing COD-fan audience ([Rolimon's](https://www.rolimons.com/game/8054462345), [earnaldo](https://earnaldo.com/blog/michaels-zombies-vs-big-paintball-2)).
- **Zombie Attack (Right Left Studios):** 1.6B visits, 94.3% rating; PvE wave shooter; 12 gamepasses (regional pricing since Apr 2026); community notes "playing as zombie" passes feel low-value ([Rolimon's](https://www.rolimons.com/game/1240123653), [Gamepasses wiki](https://zombie-attack-roblox.fandom.com/wiki/Gamepasses)).
- **Undead Defense Tycoon:** 272M visits but tycoon genre — not a direct comp ([Rolimon's](https://www.rolimons.com/game/5670292785)).

**Market gap this concept hits:** nobody big currently combines MM2's *hidden-role tension* with the COD Infected *conversion snowball* at 30-player scale with real gunplay. Existing infection games reveal zombies immediately; MM2 hides the killer but disarms the crowd. The "secretly infected until first kill" beat is the differentiator.

## 2. Design lessons (cross-game synthesis)

- **Hidden-role reveal:** MM2 reveals role privately at round start ("You are the MURDERER" card) and publicly only through action; end-of-round screen names murderer/sheriff and winner. Copy the private role card + dramatic end-screen reveal with round MVP stats. During the "stealth phase," give the infected a visible-only-to-them timer/objective so they don't idle.
- **Balancing 1-vs-29 at start:** COD gives Patient Zero one-hit melee + extra speed ([Activision Mode Recon](https://blog.activision.com/uk/en/call-of-duty/2019-12/Mode-Recon-Infected)); Flee the Facility gives survivors a 15s head start and handicaps the Beast's mobility after attacks. For 30p: first infected needs one-hit knife kill (thrown + melee), +15–25% sprint, and either extra HP or a short damage-resist window on reveal; converted zombies get weaker versions. COD's key snowball guard: **each infection adds time to the clock** (2:30 base +30s/kill) so survivors can't just run out a short timer, and the horde grows to compensate.
- **Anti-camping:** COD's answer — last survivor gets **periodic radar sweeps exposing their position**; MW3 map design gave defensible-but-breachable holdout spots (fun, not degenerate, because zombie numbers eventually overwhelm). Roblox devforum consensus: spawn protection = brief forcefield/invulnerability + spawn-area no-damage zones + don't spawn near enemies ([devforum](https://devforum.roblox.com/t/how-does-one-make-an-effective-anti-spawn-killing-system/3266905)). Zombies should respawn instantly near the action (not in one spot — avoids survivor spawn-farming).
- **Last-survivor mechanics:** COD MW3: 30 kills as uninfected survivor unlocks a Tactical Nuke that ends the match ([CoD wiki](https://callofduty.fandom.com/wiki/Infected_(gamemode))) — translate as an over-the-top "Last Stand" reward (minigun/juggernaut buff + position ping). Highlight the final survivor with a spectator camera + bounty announcement.
- **Round pacing norms:** COD Infected 2:30 + 30s/infection; STK 3–4 min; Piggy 5–15; Frosted Infection 10 max. For 30 players: **~5–6 min rounds + 20–30s intermission with map voting** is the sweet spot; longer feels like a chore when you die early — unless death = instant zombie respawn, which is why conversion (not elimination) is the genre's secret weapon: nobody spectates.
- **Economy patterns:** small per-action payouts (Piggy: 5/kill, 15/win; devforum norms 10–50 coins/kill with killstreak multipliers), win bonuses 2–3x kill value, XP → level → cosmetic unlocks (STK/FTF), 2x-coins and XP-boost gamepasses as top sellers, coins purchasable via dev products, crate/card-flip reveal for knife skins, and rarity tiers deep enough to sustain trading. Survivor kill of a zombie should pay less than infected converting a survivor (conversion is rarer per-capita); "survived full round" and "first blood as infected" bonuses drive both roles.

## 3. Technical best practices (2025–2026)

- **Server size: yes, 30 is trivially supported.** Any developer can set max players up to **200** (Game Settings → Places → Max Players); 700 is available via beta enrollment. 30 is a plain dropdown/custom value ([devforum announcement](https://devforum.roblox.com/t/experience-join-improvements-server-size-join-queues-and-social-slots-reservations/2294621)). Devforum reliability threads confirm 30–50 player servers are comfortably stable if replication is disciplined. Also from that announcement: **join queues** and **social slot reservations** exist — consider reserving 1–2 social slots so friends can join.
- **Workflow:** Rojo 7 (`rojo serve` + Studio plugin, `default.project.json` mapping `src/` → tree), fully-scripted in `.luau` files, Git + PR flow, **Wally** for packages, **Selene** lint + **StyLua** format, **Rokit** as toolchain manager, TestEZ (or Jest-Lua) in CI via GitHub Actions ([Rojo docs](https://rojo.space/docs/v7/), [toolchain guide](https://www.oflight.co.jp/en/columns/luau-developer-toolchain-rojo-selene-wally-2026), [template repo](https://github.com/takoyakisoft/roblox-rojo-wally-template)).
- **Combat authority:** never trust client hit reports. Pattern: client fires visual-only effects instantly (prediction), sends fire event; **server raycasts** (hitscan) or validates projectile path; for client-reported hits, server checks distance, line-of-sight, weapon state, fire-rate cooldown, and ammo before applying damage; reject on any failure ([kitsblox](https://kitsblox.com/blog/server-authoritative-design-roblox), [devforum](https://devforum.roblox.com/t/making-exploiter-proof-guns/235423)). Roblox published a 2026 newsroom piece on server-authority patterns ([roblox.com](https://about.roblox.com/newsroom/2026/07/creating-responsive-cheat-resistant-games-roblox-server-authority)) — and note Roblox has been shipping **server-authoritative character movement/physics betas**; check current status at build time, since infected speed-hacking is the #1 exploit surface. Thrown knives: simulate projectile on server (or server-validated with lag compensation window); one-hit conversion is high-stakes so validate hard. Also: all role assignment, round state, conversion, and reward grants server-side; RemoteEvents treated as hostile input with type/sanity checks + rate limiting.
- **Persistence:** **ProfileStore** (loleris's successor to ProfileService — ProfileService is officially unsupported now). Session-locking prevents dupe exploits; 300s autosave default + MessagingService-based lock handoff; backwards-compatible with ProfileService data ([ProfileStore docs](https://madstudioroblox.github.io/ProfileStore/), [devforum release](https://devforum.roblox.com/t/profilestore-save-your-player-data-easy-datastore-module/3190543)). Store: coins, XP/level, owned skins, stats (kills/wins/infections), daily streaks.
- **Streaming/memory:** enable **StreamingEnabled**; StreamingMinRadius ~256, TargetRadius 512–1024; keep loaded-area instances <50k (<20k for mobile comfort); scripts must use WaitForChild/CollectionService patterns since distant parts may be nil on client ([Roblox docs](https://create.roblox.com/docs/performance-optimization/improve), [devforum](https://devforum.roblox.com/t/is-streamingenabled-good-best-ways-for-optimization/625977)). For round-based multi-map: keep maps in ServerStorage, clone active map into Workspace per round, destroy on round end (bounds memory and lets you ship many maps).
- **Monetization APIs:** gamepasses (permanent: 2x coins, XP boost, radio/emotes, extra loadout slot) via `UserOwnsGamePassAsync` + `PromptGamePassPurchase`; consumables (coin packs, crate keys, "play as first infected next round" token) via developer products with a **single centralized `ProcessReceipt`** that is idempotent (record receipt IDs) and returns `PurchaseGranted`/`NotProcessedYet` only ([Roblox docs](https://create.roblox.com/docs/production/monetization/developer-products)); **experience subscriptions** (monthly Robux, launched 2024) for a VIP tier; regional pricing is now a thing (Zombie Attack adopted it Apr 2026). Note: cross-game dev-product sales are being disabled May 30, 2026.

## 4. What makes COD Infected fun → Roblox translation

Per [Activision's Mode Recon](https://blog.activision.com/uk/en/call-of-duty/2019-12/Mode-Recon-Infected) and the [CoD wiki](https://callofduty.fandom.com/wiki/Infected_(gamemode)):
- **The snowball tension curve:** you start powerful and safe, end cornered and outnumbered. Losing = switching teams, so there's no "eliminated and bored" state, and the round's incentive structure flips mid-match (survivors turned infected now hunt their friends with insider knowledge of hiding spots). This is *the* core loop — translate 1:1.
- **Patient Zero asymmetry:** one-hit melee + speed boost vs full-loadout survivors; **uniform survivor loadouts** keep it fair (translate: fixed or lightly-customizable gun loadouts, no pay-to-win stats).
- **Timer economics:** 2:30 base, +30s per infection — survivors can't stall, infected always have a live objective. Direct copy.
- **Last-survivor drama:** periodic radar sweeps expose the final survivor (anti-camp + spectacle); MW3's 30-kill Tactical Nuke gives godlike survivors a match-ending flex. Translate: position ping every ~15s for last 1–3 survivors, kill-threshold "nuke"/juggernaut reward, all spectating zombies watch the finale.
- **Holdout spots by design:** maps intentionally include defensible corners/rooftops that are *breachable* — fun sieges, not stalemates (multiple approach angles, throwing knives arc over cover). The infected's **throwing knife is the anti-camp tool**: campers in a corner are exactly what a ranged one-hit conversion counters.
- **Weapon downgrade note:** the "loadout gets worse over time" variant is from CoD's *Gun Game*-adjacent modes/older MW3 Infected playlists, not core MW2019 Infected — core Infected keeps survivor loadouts static and instead escalates via horde growth + shrinking safe space. Recommend escalating via zombie count/classes (Frosted-style class unlocks as horde grows) rather than degrading survivor guns, which feels bad on Roblox's younger audience.

**Key synthesis:** 30p / ~5-6 min rounds (+30s per early conversion) / 20-30s map-vote intermission; hidden infected with private role card and a stealth phase before first blood; one-hit throw/melee conversion, instant respawn-as-zombie, zombie class variety as the horde grows; last-survivor radar ping + Last Stand reward; end-screen reveal with MVP stats; coins-per-action + win bonus + XP levels feeding a rarity-tiered knife/gun-skin crate economy (card-flip reveal), 2x-coin & XP gamepasses, coin dev-products, VIP subscription; Rojo+Wally+Selene+StyLua fully-scripted, server-authoritative everything, ProfileStore persistence, StreamingEnabled with per-round map cloning.
