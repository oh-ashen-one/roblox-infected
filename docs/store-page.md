## Description

🧟 ONE OF YOU IS ALREADY INFECTED.

30 players. One secret zombie. When the round starts, nobody knows who Patient Zero is — not even by looks. They hunt with one-hit throwing knives. Every kill turns a survivor into a zombie... and the horde grows.

🔫 SURVIVORS: Grab your rifle, shotgun or SMG. Watch your friends. Anyone could be the infected. Shoot the hidden zombie before they strike — if you dare risk it.

🔪 INFECTED: Blend in. Pick your moment. Throw your knife, convert the living, build your horde, and consume them all before the timer runs out.

⏱ The clock gains time with every infection. Camp and you'll be radar-pinged. Survive alone and enter LAST STAND with a minigun.

🏆 Earn coins and XP every round. Open crates for knife & gun skins — Common to CHROMA. First blood bonuses, last-survivor jackpots, win multipliers.

🗺 3 maps at launch: Abandoned Mall, Suburbia at night, Research Facility. Vote every intermission.

Inspired by classic Infected & social-deduction murder mystery games. New skins and maps every update. Play with friends — 30-player servers with join queue.
